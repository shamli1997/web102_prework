About Us

Welcome to Sea Monster!

At Sea Monster, our mission is to support and fund independent games. With a passion for creativity and innovation, we have been in operation for over 12 years, nurturing the growth of talented game developers and bringing their visions to life.

Through our crowdfunding platform, we have raised a remarkable total of $800,268 for 11 unique games. This achievement is a testament to the dedication and enthusiasm of our community of backers and game enthusiasts.

However, our journey doesn't end here. Currently, there are 7 outstanding games that remain unfunded. These projects are brimming with potential and are waiting to be realized. We believe that every game deserves a chance to shine, and we invite you to join us in making these amazing games a reality.

By contributing to our crowdfunding campaigns, you become an integral part of our mission to support independent game development. Your support not only helps fund these games, but it also encourages and empowers the talented individuals behind them.

Together, let's create a thriving ecosystem for independent games and celebrate the boundless creativity of game developers. Join us on this exciting adventure as we bring remarkable games to life and shape the future of gaming.

Thank you for being a part of Sea Monster!
